About AWAAZ

Voice of the unheard. A safe space to document personal experiences. Promoting awareness through shared stories.

What We Are

AWAAZ is an anonymous, evidence-based incident reporting and awareness platform. It's a space where people can securely document and share their personal experiences — including harassment, discrimination, misconduct, and other challenging situations.

We believe that real-world experiences deserve to be heard and documented. By providing a platform for people to share their stories, we promote awareness, encourage dialogue, and help identify patterns that deserve attention.

Important: AWAAZ is not a legal authority. All submissions are user-generated content, and we do not verify, validate, or guarantee the accuracy of any submissions. Users are fully responsible for the content they submit. Our platform is designed for awareness and documentation purposes only, and is not a replacement for law enforcement, legal proceedings, or official complaint mechanisms.

Our Motivation

We created AWAAZ because we believe that everyone deserves a space to document their experiences safely and anonymously. Too many people suffer in isolation, uncertain whether their experiences matter or whether anyone will listen.

Why This Matters:

  • Documentation Creates Awareness: By documenting experiences, we create a record that helps raise awareness about issues that might otherwise go unnoticed.
  • Patterns Help Identify Issues: When multiple people report similar experiences, patterns may emerge that help highlight broader issues deserving attention and action.
  • Anonymity Enables Safety: Many people hesitate to speak up due to fear of retaliation or judgment. Anonymity allows people to share their experiences without risk.
  • Accessible Record-Keeping: AWAAZ provides a space where experiences are securely documented and easily accessible for awareness and general reference.
  • Community Voices Matter: When communities document shared experiences, it promotes understanding and encourages dialogue about important issues.

We believe in the power of collective voices. By providing a platform where people can safely share their experiences, we create space for dialogue, awareness, and meaningful discussion about issues that affect our communities.
